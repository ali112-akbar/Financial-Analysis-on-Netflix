---

## Overview

A real-world **buy-side equity analyst workflow** for Netflix Inc. (NASDAQ: NFLX), simulating the kind of research produced at asset management firms and investment banks.

| Stage | Method |
|-------|--------|
| Financial Statement Analysis | Revenue, EBITDA, Free Cash Flow trends |
| Valuation | DCF model · EV/EBITDA · P/E comparables |
| Risk Assessment | Sensitivity analysis · Bear/Base/Bull scenarios |
| Final Verdict | Investment recommendation with price target |

---

## Analysis Highlights

**Revenue and Growth**
- Multi-year revenue trend (FY2019–FY2024)
- Subscriber growth vs. average revenue per user (ARM)
- Operating leverage as content costs stabilise

**Valuation Models**
- DCF with WACC sensitivity table
- Comparable company analysis — EV/EBITDA vs. Disney+, Amazon Prime, Warner Bros.
- Scenario analysis: Bull (ad-tier growth), Base, Bear (saturation)

**Risk Factors**
- Streaming market saturation and churn risk
- Content cost inflation and forex exposure (60%+ revenue outside the US)
- Password-sharing crackdown sensitivity

**Deliverables**
- netflix_financial_analysis.ipynb — Python notebook with charts and models
- Netflix Financial Analysis (final report).pdf — Institutional-style PDF report
- Result after execution.pdf — Output visualisations and summary tables
